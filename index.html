About Me

After 12 years in advertising I decided that it was time to try something new. Six years ago I started a job at a software company with NO previous experience in software and the learning curve was steep, difficult, and frustrating but also incredibly rewarding.


I managed a small semi-technical team responsible for importing client data into databases using the custom built tools that development created for us. I also managed the Tech Writing department. I realized that I wanted to jump deeper into the technology and decided to attend the KU Coding Bootcamp.
